Recipe Finder

This "Recipe Finder" web app let's you search different recipes based on a list of ingredients. It's fully responsive, i.e. works on almost all the major viewports, from the widest to the (possibly) the smallest. ðŸ˜€ Almost all the major edge cases and errors are handled gracefully, making the web app very user-friendly. From the returned list of recipes, you can click the button "See Recipe" to learn more by visiting the url for the recipe detials. You can click on "Add to Favorites" to add recipes to your favorites, and "Share" to share recipes with other people.


The recipes that are added to your favorites are stored in your browser's local storage (meaning, it's going to be available until you clear your browser cache and storage). Also, you can go to the Favorites Page for taking a look at all your favorites.



Note: The Recipe Puppy API is being used under the hood to handle the search queries. And all the source code for this project can be found in a github repository. And the live demo is being served by the generous folks @ Surge
